# ERP System - Detailed Project Documentation

## Cabin Management Module
Designed and implemented a multi-cabinet inventory management module supporting item transfers between cabinets with real-time stock synchronization, cost recalculation, and transactional integrity. The system maintains detailed audit logs for all transfers to ensure traceability and financial accuracy across multiple storage locations.

## System Overview
Independently designed and developed a web-based ERP system for SMEs including supermarkets, currency exchange offices, and retail shops.

## Key Modules
### 1. Accounts & Balances
- Multi-currency account management with real-time updates

### 2. Cabinet & Inventory Management
- Track items, purchases, sales, and stock transfers
- Stock forecasting and real-time synchronization
- Multi-cabinet support with audit trails

### 3. Purchases & Sales
- Manage bills, returns, and automated totals

### 4. Cash & Money Management
- Expense tracking and financial reporting

### 5. Reporting & Analytics
- Detailed inventory and financial reports
- Business intelligence dashboards

### 6. User & Role Management
- Multi-user access with role-based permissions
- Activity logs for security and tracking

### 7. Currency & Gold Rates
- Manage exchange rates and historical gold rates

## Deployment
- Deployed on Linux hosting with ongoing maintenance
- Due to commercial use, the system is not publicly accessible
- Screenshots available in the `/screenshots` folder

## Technologies
- **Backend:** Laravel, PHP
- **Frontend:** React.js, Material-UI, Redux Toolkit
- **Database:** MySQL
- **Hosting:** Linux/Apache
